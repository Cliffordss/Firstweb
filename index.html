Childhood

As a kid, I grew up as the only child in my family, never having any siblings. It could get pretty lonely sometimes, not having someone to play with when I was young. Most of the time, I'd head outside to hang out with other kids in the neighborhood. I live with both my mom and dad, and they've always been incredibly loving and caring. Although my memories of childhood are a bit fuzzy, a few things stand out. I used to be really into playing with toy cars and riding my bicycle after school. Watching DVDs was another favorite pastime of mine. My mom, who was quite religious, taught me many prayers during my early years. I remember spending time praying alone, and we consistently attended church every Sunday. When I reached fifth grade, I took a step further in my involvement with the church and became an altar server. It was a significant part of my growing up and added a unique dimension to my childhood
